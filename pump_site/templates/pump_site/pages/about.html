VISA Pvt. Ltd (Virtual Instrumentation & Software Applications Pvt. Ltd) is a Chennai-based manufacturer and supplier of industrial instrumentation and fluid handling equipment. Established with a commitment to quality and innovation, we serve industries across India.

Our peristaltic pump division offers precision-engineered pumps for pharmaceutical, chemical, food & beverage, water treatment, and laboratory applications. Every pump is designed for long life, easy maintenance, and accurate flow control.
