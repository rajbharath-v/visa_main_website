Who we are

Virtual Instrumentation & Software Applications Pvt. Ltd

Founded in 1990 and incorporated in 1999, VISA Pvt. Ltd is a Chennai-based manufacturer and supplier of precision process control instruments and fluid handling systems. We serve industries across India and export to Nepal, Bangladesh, Saudi Arabia, Indonesia, and Turkey.

Our group operates through three divisions — VISA Products, Teletherm Instruments (manufacturing), and a Software division for custom process control applications.
